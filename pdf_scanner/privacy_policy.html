Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our mobile application (the "Application").

We use your data to provide and improve the Application. By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

Personal Data

While using our Application, we do not require you to provide us with personally identifiable information to use the core features (scanning and PDF generation).

Camera and Device Storage Access

To provide its core functionality, the Application requires explicit permission to access your device’s Camera and Storage/Media Gallery.

  • Camera Access: Used solely to capture images of documents via the integrated cunning_document_scanner library.
  • Storage Access: Used to save the generated PDF files to your device or to import existing images for PDF conversion.

Important Note on Local Processing: All image processing, document edge-detection, and PDF generation happen entirely on your local device. We do not upload, host, or store your scanned documents, images, or PDFs on any external servers.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertisements

The Application features banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To display these ads, the Application integrates third-party Software Development Kits (SDKs).

Google AdMob may collect and use certain tracking data to serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements. This data may include:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID or Advertising Identifier for iOS).
  • IP address (used to infer approximate location).
  • Interaction data (e.g., ad clicks and views).

3. Data Retention and Security

Because your scanned documents and PDFs are stored locally on your device, the security of those files relies on your device's built-in security features. We do not have access to your files and cannot recover or delete them for you.

We implement standard industry measures to secure the limited data collected by our analytics or advertising partners, but please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided a third-party partner with personal data, please contact that partner or notify us so we can take necessary actions.
