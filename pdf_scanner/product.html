App Summary: Fast PDF Scanner

A user-first utility built for efficiency, privacy, and accessibility.

This mobile application is a lightweight, high-performance tool designed to capture images and instantly compile them into clean, professional PDF documents. Powered by the advanced cunning_document_scanner library, it delivers precise real-time page edge-detection and optimized document cropping natively on your mobile device.

Core Philosophy: Free, Happy, and Minimal Ads

The driving mission behind this project is to build a completely accessible tool that everyone can use reliably without the typical frustrations, friction, and hidden fees found in modern mobile utilities.

  • 100% Free & Open Utility: Every single core feature—from processing high-resolution document scans to generating and exporting multi-page PDFs—is fully available to everyone at zero cost. There are no premium tiers or feature paywalls.
  • Intentionally Minimal Advertisements: To support independent continuous development and cover backend ecosystem maintenance, the application integrates Google AdMob banner ads. However, these are kept strictly non-intrusive. The app contains absolutely no aggressive fullscreen interstitials, unskippable video ads, or workflow disruptions.
  • Strict Privacy First: Your personal data and sensitive documents belong exclusively to you. All image parsing, edge-detection, and PDF rendering take place entirely locally on your physical device hardware. No photos, scans, or document metadata are ever uploaded, monitored, or transmitted to external servers.
Enjoy a clean, fast, and completely private scanning experience keeping everyone happy and productive!
